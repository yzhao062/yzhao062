## 🌱 Research

My research focuses on **auditing, securing, and deploying reliable AI systems**, with an emphasis on **foundation models and agentic systems operating in real-world environments**.

I study how foundation models, tool-using agents, and multi-agent systems can produce **traceable evidence** of their behavior, support **meaningful audit** after deployment, satisfy **checkable safety and policy requirements**, and operate **reliably in high-stakes settings**.
